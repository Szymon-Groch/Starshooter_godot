<h2>How to run</h2>
Extract "Build_win.zip" or "Build_linux.zip"(depending on your system), then open executable file.


<h2>How to play</h2>

**Player 1:**

W - Move Up  
S - Move Down  
D - Move Right  
A - Move Left 
Space - shoot

**Score:**

Normal: +15 points
Diver: +25 points
Shooting enemy: +50 points
